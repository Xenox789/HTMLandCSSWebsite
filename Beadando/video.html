The description of the video on the website

Video: F1 2023 Opening Titles.

Description:

This video is the 2023 F1 opening title which used to be played before each race weekends broadcast. While we here the official anthem of the Formula 1, all the drivers of the current grid is introduced. A short video was filmed with each of them, in front of a LED wall, which contents and colors are related to the driver and his team. First we see the new drivers, Logan Sergaent, Oscar Piastri, Nico Hülkenberg and Nick de Vries one by one. Then we see the drivers, who participated in the previous season, in backwards according to their rankings last year. We see Alexander Albon, Zhou Guanyu, Yuki Tsunoda, Lance Stroll, Pierre Gasly, Kevin Magnussen, Valtteri Bottas, Fernando Alonso, Esteban Ocon, Lando Norris and Lewis Hamilton one by one. Then we see the top 5 drivers, Carlos Sainz, George Russell, Sergio Perez, Charles Leclerc and finally the Champion of the last year, Max Verstappen. At the end of the video we see the official red F1 logo, in front of black background.
